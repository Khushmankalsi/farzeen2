Know Your Artist

Ferzeen Mehra

I’m Ferzeen Mehra Bhasin — bridal and editorial makeup artist, based in Delhi NCR, creating elevated beauty since 2017. My work is rooted in the belief that makeup should never hide who you are — it should celebrate it.

Trained at the Daniel Bauer Academy, I bring the finesse of celebrity-level artistry to the modern bride, blending global techniques with an understanding of what truly flatters Indian features, skin tones, and wedding aesthetics.

From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations, every look I create is designed to feel timeless in photographs and unforgettable in person. It’s not about trends — it’s about making you feel entirely yourself, only more luminous.
